Class: Grill

Attributes:

* foodOnGrill (array)

* burnersOn (integer)

* propaneOn (boolean)

* chefAttending (string)

Methods:

* addFood (Changes the foodOnGrill attribute by using the push method)

* igniteBurners (changes the burnersOn attribute to have more)

* turnOnPropane (Changes propaneOn attribute to a true value)

* changeChef (changes the chef name associated with the chefAttending attribute)
